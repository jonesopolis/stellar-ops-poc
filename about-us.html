About Us

StellarOps is an IT Leadership firm focusing on B2B business in the United States. We pair our deep understanding of technology and our unique perspective from a collective 300 years in the industry to help clients run their businesses well and within budget.

Started in 2020, StellarOps recognized the need for IT Leadership by engaging directly with the Client. Our team originated from an IT Service Provider background with a long history of doing business locally but with national reach. We wanted to be different, to bring that personal touch, and provide Clients quick access to the best resources. This is how we’ll succeed together.
